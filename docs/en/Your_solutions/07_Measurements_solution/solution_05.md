# Task 05 – Percentage Uncertainty

## Problem Statement

A time measurement is recorded as

$$
t = 5.45 \pm 0.22 \text{ s}
$$

Find the percentage uncertainty.

## Theory

Percentage uncertainty is

$$
\%\text{ uncertainty} = \frac{\Delta t}{t} \cdot 100\%
$$

## Step-by-Step Solution

Substitute the values:

$$
\%\text{ uncertainty} = \frac{0.22}{5.45} \cdot 100\%
$$

$$
\%\text{ uncertainty} \approx 4.04\%
$$

## Final Result

The percentage uncertainty is

$$
4.0\%
$$

## Interpretation

The uncertainty is about 4% of the measured time value.
