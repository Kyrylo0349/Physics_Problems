# Task 06 – Instrument Precision

## Problem Statement

A digital thermometer reads

$$
25.4^\circ\text{C}
$$

Assume the uncertainty is half the value of the last digit.

Determine the absolute uncertainty.

## Theory

If the smallest displayed increment is

$$
0.1^\circ\text{C}
$$

then half of that is taken as the instrumental uncertainty.

## Step-by-Step Solution

The last digit represents

$$
0.1^\circ\text{C}
$$

So the uncertainty is

$$
\Delta T = \frac{0.1}{2} = 0.05^\circ\text{C}
$$

## Final Result

$$
T = 25.4 \pm 0.05^\circ\text{C}
$$

## Interpretation

The thermometer display does not justify more precision than about half of the smallest shown increment.
