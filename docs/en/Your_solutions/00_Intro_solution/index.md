# KaTeX test

The cross product in component form is

$$
\vec{a} \times \vec{b} = \begin{pmatrix}
a_y b_z - a_z b_y \\
a_z b_x - a_x b_z \\
a_x b_y - a_y b_x
\end{pmatrix}
$$

The angle $\theta$ between two vectors is

$$
\cos \theta = \frac{\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}}{|\vec{a}| |\vec{b}|}
$$
