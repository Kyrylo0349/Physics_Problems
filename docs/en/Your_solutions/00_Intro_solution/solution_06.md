# Task 06 – Function Analysis of a Quadratic

## Problem Statement

Consider the function

$$
f(x) = 3x^2 - 12x + 7
$$

Identify any local maxima or minima.

## Theory

A quadratic function

$$
f(x) = ax^2 + bx + c
$$

has a turning point at its vertex. The $x$-coordinate of the vertex is

$$
x = -\frac{b}{2a}
$$

If

$$
a > 0
$$

the parabola opens upward and the vertex is a minimum. If

$$
a < 0
$$

the parabola opens downward and the vertex is a maximum.

## Step-by-Step Solution

For the function

$$
f(x) = 3x^2 - 12x + 7
$$

the coefficients are

$$
a = 3, \quad b = -12, \quad c = 7
$$

### 1. Find the vertex location

Use the vertex formula:

$$
x = -\frac{b}{2a}
$$

Substitute the coefficients:

$$
x = -\frac{-12}{2 \cdot 3}
$$

$$
x = \frac{12}{6}
$$

$$
x = 2
$$

### 2. Find the function value at the vertex

Evaluate the function at $x = 2$:

$$
f(2) = 3(2)^2 - 12(2) + 7
$$

$$
f(2) = 3 \cdot 4 - 24 + 7
$$

$$
f(2) = 12 - 24 + 7
$$

$$
f(2) = -5
$$

### 3. Determine whether it is a maximum or minimum

Since

$$
a = 3 > 0
$$

the parabola opens upward. Therefore the vertex represents a minimum.

## Final Result

The function has a local minimum at

$$
(2, -5)
$$

There is no local maximum.

## Interpretation

Because the parabola opens upward, the point

$$
(2, -5)
$$

is not only a local minimum but also the global minimum of the function. The graph decreases until $x = 2$ and increases afterward.







# Task 06 – Mini Theory: Function Analysis

## What this topic is about

Function analysis studies how a function behaves, especially where it increases, decreases, or reaches extreme values.

A quadratic function has the form

$$
f(x) = ax^2 + bx + c
$$

Its graph is a parabola.

## Key ideas

If $a > 0$, the parabola opens upward and has a minimum.

If $a < 0$, the parabola opens downward and has a maximum.

The turning point is called the vertex.

## Why it matters in physics

Many physical relationships are quadratic. Position under constant acceleration, potential energy, and optimization problems often involve finding maxima or minima of functions.
